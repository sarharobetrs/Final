The Basics

Card making began as early as the 1400s. This started as a way to exchange cards or sell them for certain needs. While a lot of greeting cards and blank cards are often bought in store, making them by hand is an art many people overlook. This is a simple hobby anyone can take up. All you need is some paper, a pen, and a little bit of patience; the three P's!
